Attended the 5th Asia‑Pacific Ocular Imaging Society (APOIS) Congress held at the Shangri‑La Hotel in Beijing, China. APOIS is a leading academic society dedicated to advancing ocular imaging science and clinical application across the Asia‑Pacific region, providing a platform for researchers and clinicians to exchange insights on cutting‑edge ocular imaging technologies and artificial intelligence in ophthalmology. This conference featured keynote lectures, panel discussions, workshops, and collaborative sessions on emerging trends in ocular imaging, AI‑driven diagnostics, and global eye care innovation.
